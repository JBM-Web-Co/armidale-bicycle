# JBM Web Co — Brand Guidelines

- Australian web design agency
- Builds and sells high-converting landing pages for local service businesses
- Production-grade commercial product — not a portfolio or hobby project

## Target Audience

Australian local service business owners in industries including:

- Pest control
- Electricians
- Physiotherapy clinics
- Landscaping businesses

## Tone of Voice

- Direct and commercial — get to the point
- Conversion-focused — every sentence earns its place
- No fluff, no filler, no corporate speak
- Australian English spelling (e.g. "colour", "organise", "recognised")
- Speak to business owners, not consumers
- Lead with outcomes and value, not features

## Copy Rules

- Use short sentences and plain language
- Avoid jargon unless it's industry-specific and the audience will recognise it
- CTAs should be action-oriented and specific ("Get your free quote" not "Submit")
- Headlines should address the reader's problem or desired outcome directly
